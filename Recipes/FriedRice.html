Golden Fried Rice

A delicous dish common in Asia. Very simple ingredients with infinite variations

Ingredient List

  • Cold Rice
  • Scallions
  • Garlic
  • 1 Egg per person
  • Bak Kwa

Steps

  1. Over low-medium heat, sear the sliced garlic in oil
  2. Add the Bak Kwa
  3. Add the rice and stir, increase heat to high and ensure garlic doesn't burn
  4. Add egg and mix it up, salt and pepper to liking
  5. Scallions for garnish, serve
